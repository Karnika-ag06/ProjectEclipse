University

Grant university,was established in the year 1976 with a specific objective-

"To establish and incoporate a teaching-cum-affiliating University at California for the encouragement of interdisciplinary higher educationand research with speceal emphasis on studies of Life Sciences and Environmental and Ecological Sciences".During the last year 26 years of its existences,the university has achieved a remarkable degree of success in its expansion programs,infrastructural developments,and academic excellence.

There are 66 Boards of Studies for developing and designing courses in different disiciplines.

In addition to the main Campus,there are two satellite campus-Regional Center running post-graduate teaching at California and National Law College of Texas.

As an affilating University,it has 126 institutions and colleges under its control with an enrolment of about 170,000 students.

Affilated institutes of Grant University consist of a number of Engineering Colleges ,Computer and Management Studies institutes,and pharmaceutical Colleges.
